Men Do Cry

The Origin Story

"Men Do Cry" is a concept that aims to gauge the emotional aptitude of men, primarily ones of color. The inception of "Men Do Cry" derives from a conversation held between its creator, Maya Chisem, and a friend. The exchange between them left Maya wondering- are men dealing with their emotions?

Collaborating with videographer extraordinaire, Louis Halley, the duo of Maya and Louis will present you with the scintillating visual journey that will become "Men Do Cry".

It's time to do a temperature check fellas.

About Maya

Maya's passion for photography began at a young age and has only continued to grow. Initially starting her career as more of a hobby, Maya has worked her way up to become a successful freelancer. Now with over six years of experience in the industry, Maya has honed her talents and has worked with some of the biggest names in photography, including Milk Production Studios.

Maya's use of natural light and carefully selected locations enhance the mood and emotion of her images, creating a truly memorable and personal experience for her clients and appreciator of her photos alike.

About Louis

Louis Halley currently works as a video producer for Warner Music Group- the first to be hired in this position at the company. On set, he directs + serves as a camera operator. Offset, he is the post-production editor. Louis also works as a Freelancer and has for over 5 years now, producing over 100 projects, both large and small.

His creative vision and versatility have been an asset on projects as an independent filmmaker and have given him opportunities to shoot for prolific brands such as Nike and the NBA.
